Cover for temporary accommodation if your home becomes unliveable

If your home becomes unliveable after an insured incident, QBE can help with the cost of temporary accommodation while it is being repaired or rebuilt. For eligible customers, this can be up to 24 months, or 20% of your buildings sum insured - whichever is less. QBE can also help with the reasonable cost of relocating, returning home, and some extra living expenses while you are staying elsewhere.
