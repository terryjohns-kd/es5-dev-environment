REPTILE CENTRE 

­

We are excited to announce that the Reptile Centre is now fully complete! Our latest additions include fascinating venomous snakes, a freshwater crocodile, green tree frogs and a diverse array of lizards.

New Conservation Program

KOALA CORRIDORS

­
­

We’re teaming up with the Westernport Biosphere Foundation to create tree corridors for the region's koalas.


These green pathways will help them travel safely and boost their genetic diversity.

­

This ambitious corridor of trees will connect areas of native habitat ensuring the bigger patches remain within safe and secure walking distance of each other.

­

Aquatic Fauna Facility

We have successfully obtained a grant from the Vic Government to provide a breeding facility to support populations of threatened or ecologically important freshwater fish species and provide a rescue facility for fish in emergencies such as bush fires. 

The initial focus will be on the threatened Eastern Dwarf Galaxias.

 

HAPPY BIRTHDAY LOUIE!

­

Louie, our spot-tailed quoll, celebrated his second birthday last week! 

Louie isn’t just any quoll; he’s a superstar in our Conservation in Action show, captivating audiences with his clever antics.

­

Did you know spot-tailed quolls, also known as tiger quolls, are the largest and most uniquely patterned of all quolls? They’re the only quoll species with spots on their tails, and each quoll sports a one-of-a-kind polka-dot pattern. This unique feature helps our keepers identify and care for each individual quoll with ease.
